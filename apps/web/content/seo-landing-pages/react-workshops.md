1. Question: Can you customize a workshop for our specific challenges?
   Answer: Absolutely. I often create custom workshops based on a team's codebase and challenges. We'll discuss your needs and I'll design content that addresses them directly.

2. Question: What if our team has mixed experience levels?
   Answer: I design exercises with extension challenges for advanced participants. Pairing developers of different levels often works well too.

3. Question: Do you offer ongoing training after the workshop?
   Answer: Yes. Many teams combine workshops with ongoing consulting or mentorship to reinforce learning and tackle implementation challenges.

4. Question: Can we use our own codebase for exercises?
   Answer: For custom workshops, yes. I'll review your code beforehand and design exercises that apply patterns directly to your architecture.

5. Question: What about workshops for technologies other than React?
   Answer: My workshop expertise is React/Next.js focused. For other technologies, I can recommend colleagues who specialize in those areas.
