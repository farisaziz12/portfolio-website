## FAQ Group

**faqTitle**:
Frequently Asked Questions

**faqs** (add each as an item):

1. Question: Do you only work with Stripe?
   Answer: Stripe is my specialty, but I also have experience with payment orchestration platforms and can advise on multi-provider strategies. If you're evaluating payment providers, I can help with that decision.

2. Question: Can you help with PCI compliance?
   Answer: I help teams implement Stripe in PCI-compliant ways (using Stripe Elements, Checkout, etc.). For formal PCI audits, you'll need a QSA, but I can ensure your implementation follows best practices.

3. Question: What about payment fraud?
   Answer: I help implement Stripe Radar rules and design systems that balance fraud prevention with customer experience. For high-risk businesses, I can advise on additional fraud prevention strategies.

4. Question: Do you work with marketplace/platform payments?
   Answer: Yes. Stripe Connect (Standard, Express, Custom) is complex territory. I've helped platforms design payout systems, handle KYC flows, and manage multi-party transactions.

5. Question: How long does a typical payment integration take?
   Answer: It depends on complexity. A straightforward checkout can be done in days. Subscription billing with all edge cases might take weeks. We'll scope it properly in our discovery call.
